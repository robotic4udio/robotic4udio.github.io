I have composed original music for two episodes of the historical audio walk "Fra Strandeng til Fristad. -Historiske scenarier i området omkring Pusherstreet" ("From Beach Meadow to Free Haven – Historical Scenarios in the Area Around Pusher Street"), produced by Sofie Hjorth and Mette Post.

The podcast takes the listener on a journey through time in the area now known as Pusher Street in Christiania. Through atmospheric narratives and soundscapes, the layers of history unfold – from beach meadows and prairies to the free haven's turbulent past.



Specifically, I have composed music for the following episodes:
-  **Episode 1**: Prærien ("The Prairie")
    The music supports the storytelling about the free haven's "wild west" period, when hash sales flourished freely until the yellow line was drawn in 1989. I have created an atmospheric and time-worn sonic universe that blends historical clips with original compositions. Listen here:

Through music and storytelling, "Fra Strandeng til Fristad" ("From Beach Meadow to Free Haven") brings the listener closer to the many stories that have shaped Christiania – from its origins as a beach meadow to its development as a free haven and its continued significance today. It is a unique opportunity to experience history in a new and engaging way through the power of sound.
